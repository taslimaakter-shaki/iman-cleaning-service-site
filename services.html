Cleaning service FAQs

Quick answers before you book a house cleaning, deep cleaning, move-out cleaning, or commercial cleaning in NYC.

Do you bring cleaning supplies?

Yes. Our team brings the cleaning supplies needed for your booked service. If you prefer a specific product, tell us before the visit.

Are you insured?

Yes. Iman Cleaning Service is insured and handles residential and commercial cleaning with a professional, detail-focused team.

How much does cleaning cost in NYC?

Standard cleaning starts at $180, deep cleaning starts at $280, and move-in or move-out cleaning starts at $280. Hourly specialty services are scoped before booking so you are not guessing at an open-ended total.

How long does a deep cleaning take?

Deep cleaning time depends on the size and condition of the space. We review the details first and stay focused until the agreed scope is done right.

Do you clean apartments before moving out?

Yes. Move-out cleaning is one of our core services and can include cabinets, kitchen areas, bathrooms, floors, trim, and other landlord-ready details.

Can I book same-day or weekend cleaning?

We work 7 days a week from 6AM to 11PM. Same-day and weekend availability depends on the schedule, so calling or texting is fastest.

Do you serve all five boroughs?

Yes. We clean homes and businesses in Queens, Manhattan, Brooklyn, the Bronx, and Staten Island.

Can I text photos for a quote?

Yes. Photos help us understand the size, condition, and priorities so we can send a clearer quote faster.
