Overview

Extreme Cleaning is designed for homes that have significant dirt buildup, excessive clutter, large amounts of trash, neglected conditions, strong odors, excessive dust, heavy grease buildup, or areas that have not been cleaned for an extended period of time.

If a property requires more than a standard Deep Cleaning, this service may be necessary.

Photos are required before booking so we can properly assess the condition of the property and recommend the appropriate number of cleaners and hours.
