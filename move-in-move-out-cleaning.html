Overview

Our Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning service is designed for empty homes and apartments before or after a move. This service is ideal when you are preparing to move into a new space, moving out of a rental, selling a home, or getting a property ready for the next occupant.

Important Requirement: The home must be completely empty before the cleaning appointment. Personal belongings, furniture, boxes, and large trash items should be removed before our team arrives.

If the home was recently painted, renovated, repaired, or had construction work done, please let us know before the appointment. Post-renovation dust and debris may require extra time and an additional charge.

General Cleaning Includes

  • Dusting reachable surfaces
  • Vacuuming floors, carpets, and rugs
  • Sweeping and mopping hard floors
  • Cleaning baseboards
  • Removing reachable cobwebs
  • Cleaning inside closets
  • Cleaning inside cabinets
  • Cleaning inside drawers
  • Cleaning interior windows where reachable and safe
  • Wiping light switches and door handles
  • Emptying small trash left behind
  • Removing up to 1-2 small bags of trash if needed
  • Cleaning empty rooms, hallways, and common areas

Standard service includes removing a small amount of trash. Large amounts of trash, furniture, boxes, construction debris, or junk removal are not included and must be requested in advance.

Kitchen Cleaning Includes

  • Cleaning countertops
  • Cleaning sink and faucet area
  • Cleaning stovetop surface
  • Cleaning inside oven
  • Cleaning inside refrigerator
  • Cleaning inside freezer
  • Cleaning inside microwave
  • Wiping outside of appliances
  • Cleaning inside cabinets
  • Cleaning inside drawers
  • Wiping cabinet doors and drawer fronts
  • Wiping backsplash
  • Sweeping and mopping floor

Heavy grease buildup may require extra time or an additional fee. Old stains, rust, burn marks, hard-water stains, or permanent discoloration may not be fully removable. Appliances must be empty before cleaning.

Bathroom Cleaning Includes

  • Cleaning and sanitizing toilet
  • Cleaning sink and countertop
  • Cleaning mirrors
  • Cleaning bathtub or shower surface
  • Scrubbing shower walls
  • Scrubbing tub area
  • Wiping bathroom fixtures
  • Cleaning vanity exterior
  • Cleaning inside empty bathroom cabinets and drawers
  • Removing light to moderate soap residue
  • Sweeping and mopping floor

Heavy soap scum, hard-water stains, rust, mold, old grout discoloration, or permanent stains may not be fully removable. Mold remediation is not included. We clean visible and safely accessible bathroom areas.

Empty Rooms, Bedrooms & Living Areas Include

  • Dusting reachable surfaces
  • Cleaning baseboards
  • Cleaning inside empty closets
  • Cleaning inside empty drawers if built-in
  • Wiping windowsills and reachable ledges
  • Removing reachable cobwebs
  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs
  • Sweeping and mopping hard floors
  • Wiping high-touch areas
  • Removing small trash left behind
