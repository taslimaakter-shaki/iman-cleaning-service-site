Overview

Post-Construction Cleaning is designed for properties that need cleaning after renovation, construction, repairs, remodeling, painting, or contractor work. This service focuses on removing construction dust from accessible surfaces, cleaning floors, wiping surfaces, cleaning kitchens and bathrooms, and preparing the property for move-in, staging, or daily use.

Before scheduling, please confirm that construction work is complete or provide accurate information if other workers will still be on-site. If contractors are still working, the cleaning may need to be rescheduled or billed hourly because dust and debris can continue to spread.

Photos are required if you want a flat-rate estimate.
