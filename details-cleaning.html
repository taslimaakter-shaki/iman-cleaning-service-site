Overview

Our Details Cleaning service is best for clients who want to choose exactly what areas or tasks need extra attention. If a regular Deep Cleaning does not cover everything you want, but you do not need an extreme or specialty cleaning service, Details Cleaning may be the right option.

You can send us photos and describe your cleaning needs as specifically as possible. This helps us estimate how many hours may be needed and schedule the right amount of time for your appointment.

This service can be booked for a full home or for specific areas such as the kitchen, bathroom, cabinets, closets, windows, baseboards, floors, or any room that needs focused attention.

  • Cleaning baseboards

  • Cleaning inside cabinets
  • Cleaning inside drawers
  • Cleaning interior windows where reachable and safe
  • Cleaning light switches and door handles
  • Removing reachable cobwebs

Standard service includes removing a small amount of trash. Large amounts of trash, junk removal, furniture removal, construction debris, or hauling are not included unless approved separately.
