Standard Cleaning

Standard Cleaning

Only for apartments that are in good condition and have already been deep cleaned. If your home is already clean and not in need of serious scrubbing or dusting, you can get pre-qualified for a basic clean by sending us pictures of your home.

🧼

General Cleaning Includes

  • Dusting, polishing, and vacuuming furniture and floors. We will not move furniture, but we will reach under it to clean.
  • Dry dusting the baseboards (deep cleaning baseboards is $50/hour).
  • Mopping the floors (with Swiffer and wet cloth. Please provide your own mop if you want us to use anything else).
  • Taking out the trash and replacing trash bags/liners.
  • Making up the beds. Please leave clean bedding on the bed if you want us to change sheets.
🚿

Detailed Kitchen & Bathroom Cleaning Services

We focus on essential cleaning tasks to keep your kitchen and bathroom clean. Custom extras are available upon request.

Bathroom Cleaning Includes

  • Cleaning and sanitizing the bathroom (scrubbing walls, tub, toilet, and sink)
  • Polishing bathroom faucets and mirrors

Kitchen Cleaning Includes

  • Doing dishes by hand or running the dishwasher (one load only)
  • Cleaning inside the microwave
  • Wiping down and polishing countertops and stovetop

Please let us know if you have more than one sink or dishwasher load. Extra charges will apply. Please do not leave expensive or extremely fragile dishes for us to clean.

🍽️

Not Included in Standard Cleaning

  • Cleaning blinds/shutters
  • Fridge/freezer cleaning
  • Fan cleaning
  • Organizing inside cabinets, closets, or drawers
  • Washing walls
  • Laundry
  • Scrubbing baseboards
  • Window cleaning

All of these can be added on at $50/hour and must be requested in advance.

Please notify us at least 2 days prior to your appointment so we can make changes to our schedule and adjust your rate accordingly.
