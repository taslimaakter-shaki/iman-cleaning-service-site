Privacy Policy

How Iman Cleaning Service LLC collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website, quote forms, live chat, phone, email, and SMS.

Effective Date

June 20, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address, quote details, photos you choose to submit, preferred appointment information, website chat messages, and other details you provide when requesting cleaning services.

How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to requests, prepare cleaning quotes, schedule services, provide customer support, process payments, send service-related communications, and improve our website and operations.

SMS and Phone Communications

If you provide a phone number and ask us to contact you, we may use that number to call or text you about cleaning quotes, live chat replies, appointment details, service updates, payment links, or customer support. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at Info@imancleaningservice.com or 929-803-4053.

No Sale or Marketing Sharing of Mobile Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share mobile opt-in information, phone numbers, or SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers to operate our website, forms, chat, messaging, payments, email, hosting, analytics, and business systems. These providers may process information only as needed to provide services to Iman Cleaning Service LLC.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect submitted information. No internet or messaging system can be guaranteed completely secure.
